OSTEOLOGICAL TRIVIA

Cubic inch for cubic inch, human bone is stronger than solid concrete. A block of bone that size can support up to 19,000 lbs.

The only bone in the human body not articulated to any other bone. It anchors the tongue and aids in swallowing and speech.

Humans are born with approximately 270 soft bones. Over time, many fuse together, resulting in 206 hard bones in adulthood.
